Dec. 4, 2103

Dear Harker Community,

I am writing to share some exciting news that marks a historic moment for The Harker School. When The Palo Alto Military Academy and Miss Harker’s School for Girls merged together and moved from Palo Alto to San Jose in 1972, the Nichols family took enormous risks in settling the school’s new home in San Jose. Tonight, more than four decades later, the San Jose City Council unanimously approved The Harker School's use permit of the 4525 Union Avenue property. This historic vote moves Harker one step closer towards owning all three of our campuses, and securing the City of San Jose as the permanent home of our wonderful community.

As you are aware, Harker plans to open a preschool in the fall of 2013 on the Union campus and later, as part of a broader strategy of unwinding from our Blackford lease, move our lower school program to Union and our middle school program to Bucknall. At that time the school will own all three of its San Jose sites, each uniquely dedicated to the needs of our students.

An effort like the securing of the Union Avenue campus is not possible without the help of a great team and the support of the community. I want to thank everyone in the Harker community who leaned in to make this historic accomplishment a reality.

Chris Nikoloff, Head of School

Tuition Set

The Harker Board of Trustees recently finalized tuition for Harker's new preschool at $22,000 for the full-day, five-days-a-week program. Extended care in the morning and afternoon, as well as a lunch program, will be offered at an additional expense, and half-day and partial-week programs will also be available. Please visit our preschool page for more information on the choices.

Thanks again for your interest in our proposed preschool project. We will continue to send you updates as our plans progress! Please contact us at preschool@harker.org with your questions.
