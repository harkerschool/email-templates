2013 Harker Fashion Show - Mission: Possible

Sponsorship Opportunites

This year marks the 10th anniversary of the fashion show. The committee and many members of our community are dedicating a huge amount of effort to deliver a totally unique and spectacular show for our guests to enjoy.

This year’s theme, “Mission: Possible - Dare to Try,“ is inspiring us to dream big and try to achieve things that will take the show and The Harker School to the next level of excellence, as we support our programs, our Mission and our Philosophy.

So far we have dared ourselves in these ways:
  • We are making it possible for more students than ever to participate in the show.
  • Our producers and performing arts department are working passionately and diligently to offer what will be, without a doubt, an unforgettable and visionary show.
  • Our parent community has stepped up once more to share their talents by being part of the committees.
Helping us to achieve these milestones are our many sponsors, to whom we are very grateful. I would like to invite you to consider becoming a sponsor and/or underwriter of this year’s show; join us in the rewarding journey to strive for a higher quality experience for our students.

If you are interested in being an underwriter for this year’s show, please visit our website.

I wish you a wonderful break and an extraordinary 2013!

Teré Aceves
Director of K-8 Volunteer Programs and Event Fundraising
Email: terea@harker.org
