Ways to Support the Show

This year’s theme, “Mission: Possible - Dare to Try,“ is inspiring us to dream big and achieve things that will take the show and The Harker School to the next level of excellence, while at the same time supporting our programs, our mission and our philosophy.

This year we are making it possible for more students than ever to participate in the show; our producers and performing arts department are working passionately and diligently to offer what will undoubtedly be an unforgettable and visionary show.

Helping us to achieve these milestones are our many sponsors, to whom we are very grateful. I would like to invite you to consider becoming a sponsor and/or underwriter of this year’s show; join us in the rewarding journey to strive for a higher quality experience for our students.

If you are interested in being a sponsor or underwriter for “Mission Possible - Dare to Try”, please contact Tere Aceves at terea@harker.org.

I wish you an extraordinary 2013!
