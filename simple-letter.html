We're so excited about getting the keys to our beautiful new campus this week, which will be the home of our new proposed preschool next fall (and our lower school in the future). We're planning on jumping right in with the early phases of construction!

Before we do, we thought it would be fun for families to take a peek at the site, have some coffee and pastries, tour through the buildings, check out our plans for the space, roam the grounds, meet us and ask questions. Due to state regulations our admission process cannot begin until licensing is complete (we anticipate completion in late June), but we know you're eager to envision where your children may be spending their days in the future.

Tuition Set

The Harker Board of Trustees recently finalized tuition for Harker's new preschool at $22,000 for the full-day, five-days-a-week program. Extended care in the morning and afternoon, as well as a lunch program, will be offered at an additional expense, and half-day and partial-week programs will also be available. Please visit our preschool page for more information on the choices.

Thanks again for your interest in our proposed preschool project. We will continue to send you updates as our plans progress! Please contact us at preschool@harker.org with your questions.
